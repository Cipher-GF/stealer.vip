Nitro sniper
    NightyGo [Free]
        - Fastest sniper in existence
        - Multi-Token support
            -> Snipe on multiple accounts and redeem on main
        - Webhook support
        - Toast notifications (Windows notifications)
        - Sound notifications
        - Filter
        - Cache saving (not checking same code twice)
        - Written in golang
        - Can be accessed with selfbot command (nightygo)
    Selfbot sniper:
        - Multiple token support:
            -> Checks for nitro codes on multiple accounts and redeems them on your main account.
        - Save codes in cache to not check them twice (15 minutes).
        - Works on a different thread for faster response times.
        - Filter codes who are less than 16 characters long.
        - Ignore codes with blacklisted words such as nitro or sniper.
